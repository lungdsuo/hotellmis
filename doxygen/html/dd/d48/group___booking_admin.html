Guest check in or registration can come directly from the reservation data, if no reservation exists a registration can occur at time of checkin.
If the guest is a new guest (does not have a profile recorded) then on filling in all details for registration a new profile will automatically be created.

By default check in times are set to 14:00 (2pm) check in and 10:00 (10am) check out, this can be altered by selecting the calendar icon and modifying the time from the calendar popup window.
The window on the right side will reflect the summary of the information for the registration and rates applied.
