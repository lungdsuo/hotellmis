The window on the right side will reflect the summary of the information for the registration and rates applied.

Reservation List
Click the List button to go to the Reservation List.
Can search here by status, by arrival/departure dates, by guest name or by voucher number.
Select the magnifying glass icon beside a specific reservation to open and view/edit that reservation.
Select the bed icon to check in a reservation and go from reservation to guest registration/check in.
Select the open door icon to go directly to invoicing - in case of cancellation fees etc.
Select the dust bin icon to delete the reservation.
