This directory contains files used to connect the OTA Hotel Management software to the
e-Bridge by e-Novate Pte Ltd.

As the e-Bridge and OTA SDK are closed source license the files that link to the e-Bridge are not
part of the distribution.

If you would like to have your distribution of OTA Hotel Management to be connected to the 
e-Bridge please contact e-Novate sales@e-novate.com.sg
